Wnat is the problem?

  • The underrepresentation of women in STEM careers, particularly in engineering, physical science, and computing, is leading to a lack of diversity in STEM products and services, making them incongruous with their intended audience.

Why do I care?

  • I am female and plan to work in computer science.
  • I believe in equal representation of all people in every field and the idea that all people regardless of gender are equally capable of obtaining a career in a STEM field.
